How to Play

PrepCombo: clinical combos against the clock

1

Long press a card to flip it and read its clinical hint.

2

Tap a card to select it. Gold border appears. Tap again to deselect.

3

Select exactly 3 cards, then tap the right Subject below.

4

Match 3 cards with the same hidden tag to unlock HOD Status and a Medical Treasure.

5

3 turns per round. 3 rounds. 120 seconds per round.

+

Scoring: correct placements earn points. A 3-card tag match multiplies your score 20x. Submit faster for a speed bonus on top.

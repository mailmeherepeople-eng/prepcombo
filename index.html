HOW TO PLAY

PrepCombo — clinical combos against the clock

1

Double tap a card to select it. A gold border appears. Double tap again to deselect.

2

Long press a card to flip it and read its clinical hint.

3

Select exactly 3 cards, then tap the right Specialty Bucket.

4

Match 3 cards with the same hidden tag to unlock HOD Status and a Medical Treasure.

5

3 turns per round. 3 rounds. 60 seconds per round. Card colours appear in the last turn as a hint.

$

Scoring: correct bucket placements earn points. A 3-card tag match multiplies your score by 20x. The faster you submit, the more speed bonus points you earn on top. Streaks of good combos are tracked and rewarded.
